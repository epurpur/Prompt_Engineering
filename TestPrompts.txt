Bad- Can you please give me some of the reasons why covid vaccines are effective?\

L - Logical. Structured and coherent prompts\
Bad- Give me a recipe to cook hamburgers for dinner tonight.\
Good- List the steps to cook hamburgers. Start with food prep and end with dinner\
\
E - Explicit. Clear output specifications\
Bad- What are the causes of the war in Ukraine?\
Good- How did the war in Ukraine start? What events specifically in the last ten years led up to it.\

A - Adaptive. Flexibility and customization\
